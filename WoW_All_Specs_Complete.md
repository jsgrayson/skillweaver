## **ARMS WARRIOR**

### Hero Talent Options
- **Slayer** (Raid Single-Target): Execute damage focus + mobility
- **Colossus** (M+ Cleave): Sustained bleeds + cleave damage
- **Demolish** (Alternative): Crowd control focus

---

### RAID BUILD - Slayer Hero Talent
**Key Talents:**
- Charge (gap closer + opener)
- Mortal Strike (primary ability)
- Colossus Smash (execute setup)
- Warbreaker (alternative CD)
- Rending Slam (applies Rend)
- Cleave (AoE threat + damage)
- Execute (final hit ability)
- Sweeping Strikes (passive cleave)
- Overpower (interrupt + damage)
- Skullsplitter (cooldown reduction)
- Slayer Hero Talents: Execute buffs, mobility, crit damage

**Rotation Priority (Single-Target):**
1. Charge/Intervene (pull opener)
2. Keep Rend applied (via Rending Slam or passive)
3. Mortal Strike on cooldown
4. Colossus Smash before Execute
5. Execute on low-health targets (Slayer bonus)
6. Overpower for rotation flow
7. Auto-attacks filler

**Stat Priority:**
- Mastery > Haste > Critical Strike > Versatility

**Playstyle Focus:**
- Execute damage is primary (Slayer focus)
- Rend uptime = damage increase
- Colossus Smash debuff = Execute boost
- Use Slayer mobility for mechanic avoidance
- Pool rage for Execute windows

**Sources**: Icy-Veins Arms Warrior[101], Method.gg[95]

# HOW TO IMPORT TALENT BUILDS & WOWHEAD STRINGS

## Step-by-Step Import Process

1. **Find Talent Build**: Visit **Icy-Veins.com**, **Method.gg**, or **Wowhead.com**
   - Navigate to your class → specialization
   - Scroll to "Export Talents" button at bottom of talent calculator

2. **Copy the String**: The export string will look like:
   ```
   BAoABAo/EDABCAwQRBgIAAjZmZmZmZmxMDWAMzgZMAMzmJmZmZm5MTzYmZmZmZmxMDLGwGAMDEwzDEJAagMzgZmZmJmZmZmZGxMAMwGwAxMAMzAgZmZmZmZmZmZnBoAzMDczgZmZmZmZmFoxmA3ODmZmBmZnBmZnBmZmZmxMAMzgzYGAhYWBmZmZmZmZmZmhggOzGwAGAA=
   ```

3. **Open WoW Talent Menu**: 
   - Press **N** to open talent interface
   - Select specialization first

4. **Import the String**:
   - Look at bottom of talent window for **Import** button
   - Click **Import**
   - Paste the string (Ctrl+V)
   - Name your loadout (e.g., "Raid ST", "M+ Push")
   - Click **Import** to confirm
   - Click **Accept** when ready

5. **Save Your Loadout**:
   - Automatically saved once imported
   - Switch between loadouts instantly
   - Create multiple per spec for different content

# FINAL OPTIMIZATION TIPS FOR DPS SPECS

## Universal DPS Excellence
1. **Perfect Your Rotation**: Practice opener + rotation until muscle memory
2. **Minimize Downtime**: Always casting, movement between globals
3. **Cooldown Alignment**: Sync burst CDs with raid/dungeon phases
4. **Log Review**: Compare to top players in your class/item level
5. **Adapt Talents**: Swap builds per encounter, not generic one-size-fits-all
6. **Stat Optimization**: Sim your character (SimC) for gear/stat priorities
7. **Positioning Mastery**: Optimal positioning = DPS uptime + mechanic avoidance

## Spec-Specific Priority
- **Execute Specs** (Arms, Assassination, Subtlety Rogue): Time bursts for low-HP phases
- **DoT Specs** (Shadow Priest, Balance Druid, Assassination): Maintain full uptime between phases
- **Proc-Heavy Specs** (Enhancement Shaman, Windwalker Monk): Track procs with WeakAuras
- **Cooldown-Dependent** (Destruction Warlock, Eye Beam Havoc): Align CDs with raid timeline
- **Resource Management** (Fury Warrior, Windwalker Monk): Pool resources before burst
